Omurice Recipe

Description

A Japanese dish consisting of an omelette made with fried rice and thin, fried scrambled eggs, usually topped with ketchup.

Ingredients

  1. Vegetable oil
  2. Cooked rice
  3. Green onion
  4. Onion
  5. Mushrooms
  6. Carrots
  7. Chicken
  8. Eggs
  9. Milk
  10. Soy Sauce
  11. Ketchup
