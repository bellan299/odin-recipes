Nikujaga (肉じゃが)

Description

With chunks of potatoes, onion, and thinly sliced beef simmered in savory and sweet dashi broth, Japanese Meat and Potato Stew (Nikujaga) is one of the most iconic home-cooked dishes in Japan.

Ingredients

  1. Potatoes
  2. Thinly sliced beef or pork
  3. Onion
  4. Shirataki Noodles
  5. Carrot
  6. Green vegetables (Most commonly snow peas, green beans, or green peas)
  7. Seasonings – soy sauce, sugar, mirin, sake
